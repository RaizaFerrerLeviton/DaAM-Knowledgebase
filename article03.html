Migrate/Change Accounts

  1. Log into relevant superuser account.
  2. Click on User/Person icon in top right, select Users.
  3. Click on the username that needs changes. This will take you to the User Administration page
  4. From here, you can edit the Login, password, email, and names associated with the accountl.
  5. Click on "Apply" in the bottom right when all changes are made.
